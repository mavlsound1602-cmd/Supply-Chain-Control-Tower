# Supply Chain Control Tower Dashboard

## Overview

The Supply Chain Control Tower Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence dashboard developed using Google Sheets. It provides a centralized view of supply chain operations by integrating inventory, logistics, supplier performance, warehouse utilization, risk management, and financial KPIs into a single executive dashboard.

The dashboard enables decision-makers to monitor operational performance, identify bottlenecks, evaluate supplier reliability, optimize warehouse capacity, and assess potential business risks through data-driven insights.

---

## Business Problem

Supply chain managers often work with information spread across multiple systems, making it difficult to monitor operations efficiently.

This dashboard addresses that challenge by combining operational and financial metrics into a single decision-support platform, enabling faster and more informed business decisions.

---

## Dashboard Objectives

- Monitor overall supply chain performance
- Track logistics and delivery efficiency
- Evaluate supplier reliability
- Analyze warehouse utilization
- Identify inventory risks
- Monitor operational disruptions
- Support executive-level decision making

---

## Dashboard Modules

### Executive KPI Dashboard

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- On-Time Delivery %
- Total Shipping Cost
- Average Transit Time
- Delayed Shipments
- Total CO₂ Emissions

---

### Revenue Analytics

- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Region
- Revenue by Sales Channel

Provides insights into revenue performance across different business dimensions.

---

### Inventory Analytics

- Inventory Health Distribution
- Healthy Inventory
- Overstock Inventory
- Stockout Risk

Helps identify inventory imbalances and potential stock shortages.

---

### Supplier Performance

Measures supplier efficiency using:

- Lead Time
- OTIF (On Time In Full)
- Supplier Rating
- Defect Rate

Supports supplier evaluation and procurement decisions.

---

### Logistics Analytics

- Average Delay by Vehicle
- Shipping Cost Analysis
- Transit Time Monitoring

Provides visibility into transportation performance and delivery efficiency.

---

### Warehouse Analytics

Warehouse Utilization Analysis based on:

- Current Inventory
- Warehouse Capacity
- Utilization Percentage
- Warehouse Status

Identifies warehouses operating beyond capacity and available storage.

---

### Risk Monitoring

Tracks operational risks including:

- Supplier Delay
- Stockout
- Demand Spike
- Fuel Increase
- Quality Issue
- Transport Strike

Supports proactive risk management across the supply chain.

---

### Executive Recommendations

The dashboard concludes with automated business recommendations based on operational KPIs, allowing management to quickly identify improvement opportunities.

---

## Dataset

The dashboard was built using a synthetic enterprise-scale supply chain dataset containing:

- Orders
- Shipments
- Inventory
- Products
- Customers
- Suppliers
- Warehouses
- Risk Events

The dataset simulates real-world business operations across multiple regions and warehouses.
